Frequently asked questions

Is my data safe? Do you see my files?
Absolutely not. CleanSweep runs entirely on your computer. The AI model is downloaded once and runs locally. Your files, thumbnails, and scan results never leave your machine.
What types of files can CleanSweep scan?
Free: all common image formats (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, etc.). Pro: videos (MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV…) and documents (PDF, DOCX, PPTX, XLSX).
How accurate is the detection?
The AI model (ViT-base) achieves over 95% accuracy on standard benchmarks. You can adjust the sensitivity threshold to tune false positives vs. false negatives for your use case.
How fast does it scan?
With GPU acceleration: 10–30 images/second. On CPU: 2–5 images/second. A folder of 10,000 photos takes about 10 minutes on CPU, under 2 minutes on GPU.
Can I recover deleted files?
We recommend using Quarantine instead of Delete for reversibility. Quarantined files are moved to a _cleansweep_quarantine folder and can be restored manually.
Does it work on Mac or Linux?
Currently Windows 10/11 only. Mac and Linux versions are planned for a future release.
